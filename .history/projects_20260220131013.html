Banking Application with Predictive Analytics and Regression Analysis

Final Year project that involved building a banking application with payment features (Mpesa, Airtel and Telkom), tracking user financial patterns (income, expenses, savings) and having charts that show their spending habits. The predictive model was trained on historical data to forecast user spending patterns and to provide personalized financial insights and recommendations to users, helping them make informed decisions about their finances. This also allows the app to know what loans fit one's income and how fast they can pay it and suggets to users how reasonable a saving goal is based on their spending habbits and income.

Hiring Model

This was a project that I built in Third Year at my attachment at Zemo Limited for a client. This software was built to help a call center hire people based on call data, shifts, performance and other factors. It also had a second model that was trained on sales, profits and commissions to be met by each sales person by a certain time. The model was trained on historical data to know when a company is understaffed, departments that need shorter shifts and rotation to ensure good working conditions.
