The Founder

I love wigs and how they are so versatile and low maintenance, however I have struggles to find good quality wigs that last long and look natural. In my recent travel to India I got to see quality hair and a gap in the market for affordable, durable and natural looking wigs for other wig lovers like me. This is why I started SKRNCH to provide premium Indian human hair wigs and extensions that are affordable, durable and look natural.
